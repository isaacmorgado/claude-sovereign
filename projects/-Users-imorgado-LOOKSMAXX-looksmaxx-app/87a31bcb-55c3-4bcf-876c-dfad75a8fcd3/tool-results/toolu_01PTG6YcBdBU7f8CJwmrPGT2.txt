**Top 100 High-Protein Foods for Bodybuilders** (Dr. Muscle - February 2025)

- Greek yogurt offers around 20 grams of protein per cup, while cottage cheese provides about 28 grams of protein per cup.

**MuscleTech Guide**
- As a rule of thumb, if your goal is muscle building a good target is around a gram of protein for every pound of body weight per day.
- Chicken and turkey are popular choices in many bodybuilders' diets as they provide all the essential amino acids and can be an inexpensive option. Poultry is typically a lean source of protein.
