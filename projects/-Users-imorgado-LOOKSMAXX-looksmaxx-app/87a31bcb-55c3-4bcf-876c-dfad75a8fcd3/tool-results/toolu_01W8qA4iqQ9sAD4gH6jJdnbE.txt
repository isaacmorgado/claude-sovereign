### More Comprehensive Routines

An effective men's routine can cover five core steps: Cleanse (wash away dirt, oil, and environmental pollutants), Exfoliate (remove dead skin cells for smoother texture 2-3 times weekly), Moisturize (hydrate and strengthen your skin's natural barrier), Target (address specific concerns like aging, acne, or dark circles), and Protect (apply sunscreen - the single most effective anti-aging step).(no content)The key steps include: cleanse, treat (if necessary), moisturize, and load up on sunscreen.

### Product Layering Order

After cleansing, products in your skincare routine should be layered from thin to thick. Serums come first, followed by eye cream or any other targeted treatment, with moisturizer applied next and SPF at the end.
